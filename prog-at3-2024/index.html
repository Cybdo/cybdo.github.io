Welcome to Melbourne!

Today's Plan

Today, we will explore the city of Melbourne. We will visit the following landmarks:

  • Federation Square
  • Queen Victoria Market
  • Chinatown
  • Luna Park

9am: Breakfast

We will be having breakfast at Brick Lane Cafe, a popular cafe in Melbourne.

10am: Federation Square

We will be visiting Federation Square, a cultural precinct in the heart of Melbourne. It is home to a number of galleries, museums and restaurants.

10:30am: Queen Victoria Market

We will be visiting the Queen Victoria Market, a historic landmark in Melbourne. It is the largest open-air market in the Southern Hemisphere.

12pm: Chinatown

There are many chinese restaurants and stores in Chinatown, take a moment to appreciate the landscape and architecture, and enjoy some delicious food, ready for Luna Park.

2pm: Luna Park

Welcome to Luna Park! Luna Park is a historic amusement park located in St Kilda, Melbourne. It is home to the Scenic Railway, the oldest continually operating roller coaster in the world.

6pm: Dinner

Dinnertime is where we part. You can either enjoy dinner within Luna Park or head back to the city for a meal. There are a variety of restaurants in the city, so take your pick!

Have an active MYKI!

Be sure to have a valid MYKI card, as we will be taking public transport throughout the day. You can purchase a myki card at any train station or 7-Eleven store, such as Flinders Street, where we meet.

Enjoy your day in Melbourne!
